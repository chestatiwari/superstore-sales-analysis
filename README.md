# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
Analyzed 9,994 sales transactions from a US retail superstore 
to identify revenue trends, profitability patterns, and business 
insights using MySQL and Excel.

## Tools Used
- MySQL Workbench (Data Analysis & Querying)
- Microsoft Excel (Dashboard & Visualization)

## Business Questions Answered
1. Which product category generates the highest revenue?
2. Which products are most and least profitable?
3. How do sales trend month over month?
4. Which region performs best in sales and profit?
5. Does offering discounts hurt overall profitability?
6. Which customer segment is most valuable?

## Key Findings
- Technology leads in revenue ($835,900) across all categories
- High discounts (40%+) result in negative average profit
- West region dominates sales performance
- Monthly revenue shows consistent upward growth from 2014 to 2017
